# JoyVASA

Original Repository:
https://github.com/jdh-algo/JoyVASA

Project Page:
https://jdh-algo.github.io/JoyVASA/

Category:
Audio Driven Talking Head Animation

Strengths:

* Natural facial expressions
* Realistic head motion
* Audio-driven animation
* Strong emotion generation
* Supports human and animal portrait animation

Potential Usage:

* AI Influencer
* Digital Human
* Virtual Host
* Talking Avatar
* Emotion Driven Character

Compared To:

* More expressive than LivePortrait
* Better emotional dynamics than EchoMimicV2
* Stronger head motion generation
* Focused on face and emotion rather than hand gestures

Future Integration:

* Emotion Engine
* Facial Expression Controller
* Head Motion Generator

Dependencies:

* LivePortrait
* Wav2Vec2
* Diffusion Motion Generator

Status:
Research & Testing
