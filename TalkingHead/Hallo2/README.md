# Hallo2

Original Repository:
https://github.com/fudan-generative-vision/hallo2

Project Page:
https://fudan-generative-vision.github.io/hallo2/

Category:
Audio Driven Talking Head Generation

Strengths:

* Excellent lip synchronization
* Natural facial expressions
* High quality speech animation
* Better mouth movement than many talking-head models
* Supports long-form speech

Potential Usage:

* AI Influencer
* News Presenter
* Virtual Host
* Digital Human
* Talking Avatar

Compared To:

* Better lip sync than EchoMimicV2
* Better speech animation than LivePortrait
* Weaker upper-body and hand motion than EchoMimicV2

Future Integration:

* Speech Animation Engine
* Lip Sync Enhancement Module

Status:
Research & Testing
